About Me

Hello, my name is Sarah Randall. I currently live in Davis County, Utah with my husband and two little kids. I graduated from Utah State University with a bachelor's degree in Marketing in 2014. After that, I worked at the Huntsman Cancer Hospital and loved it there. Helping people made me feel fulfilled in my job. I also got my nail technician license for a creative outlet. My husband got a sales job offer that lead us to Cody, WY and St. George, UT. We are finally back up to Davis County and are happy to be back around family. I have been grateful to be a stay-at-home mom the past several years.

Now that my kids are older, I felt it was time for me to make a change. I attended the University of Utah Full-Stack Developer Bootcamp and finished with a 97% overall. A month prior to finishing, I landed a job as a Application Configuration Specialist at a FinTech company. My job includes working with HTML, CSS, MySQL, API, and programming languages called Clojure and Blitz. I love my job, and I have an amazing team. I've had the opportunity to mentor others and give demos on HTML and CSS to my colleagues. I find joy and satisfaction in working with HTML, CSS, and other programming languages.
